Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email Request: Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include Information: Please include your username and any relevant information that will help us identify your data.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at your-email@example.com.
